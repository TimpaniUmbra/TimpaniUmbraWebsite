A photo series using Mario Kart World's photo mode, as an excuse to further explore the tracks featured.  I realized as I was playing that the environments, beyond the usual tracks, featured a TON of additional details and secrets that better place them within the world created for this game, and I wanted to see what I could showcase within that.

These are being shared on my [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/timpaniumbra.xyz), one track per day starting on September 25, 2025.  The comments from each post will be added here as well. 
### Rules:
- 1 photo per track
- Using Rosalina, Touring outfit, riding the Mach Rocket
- No outside edits, only using the features of the game's photo mode.

>[!info] Heads Up!
>This is currently a work-in-progress project.  Every photo is prepped, but I'm sharing them once per day on [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/timpaniumbra.xyz), and will update this page after every 10 tracks posted.
