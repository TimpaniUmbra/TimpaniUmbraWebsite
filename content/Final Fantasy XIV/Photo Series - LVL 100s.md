After Dawntrail's release, one of my first goals was to level every job to 100 to meet the level cap.  Alongside this, I made a nice glamour and GPose for every job as I capped them!

I liked trying to do a variety of sets, spanning from lvl1 glamour gear to dungeon gear to artifact gear, and so on!  It was a good experiment in trying new things and forcing myself to not just rely on old glamour tactics.
