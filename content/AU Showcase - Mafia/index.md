*The city of Queensport is always hustling and bustling with activity.  When night falls, however, that activity becomes a lot more sinister.  It's when the mafia comes out to play, when the dirty deeds get done, and when the horrors become a little more real.*

*When an incident sparks a mafia conflict on a greater scale than ever before in this city, who will come out on top?  Who would you side with?  Or, would you find your own way in the chaos?*

---

To be completely honest I don't know how I ended up in this AU :)

I enjoyed watching it from afar, as it slowly started building up from [[My Wonderful Friends#Moe|Moe]], then [[My Wonderful Friends#Shiki|Shiki]], and THEN [[My Wonderful Friends#Bimu|Bimu]] as the three of them built up their little team.  It was really cool watching them craft this little brainworm (and their love of suits) into something that could be its own story!

Then I was aware of [[My Wonderful Friends#Ren|Ren]] getting involved in a really funny way, as an accidental victim of the other three's clan.  Damn.

LATER, there was a one-off joke when streaming with some of these guys about how I'd just appear and "support" both sides, basically to make some easy coin and watch the fireworks.  Knowing me it became it's own brainworm and, with the blessing of the original 3 and a sketch commission from Shiki, I was the first neutral-party character!

It's been a lot of fun seeing this little funny thing come to life in a lot of fun ways, and watching Ren craft a story out of it has been super cool!  I'm excited to see it go farther!!
